# Business Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Fraud Dollar Exposure | Estimated financial exposure associated with suspected FWA activity |
| Stopped Payment Savings | Dollars prevented from payment due to pre-payment intervention |
| Recovered Dollars | Dollars recovered after investigation |
| Provider Risk Score | Composite score based on claim behavior, exposure, detection history, and peer-group anomalies |
| Doctor Shopping | Member behavior involving multiple prescribers/pharmacies for controlled substances |
| Phantom Billing | Billing for services or prescriptions not actually provided |
| Upcoding | Billing a higher-intensity service than clinically supported |
